About us

We offer the best engineering,
health, safety and environmental Services.

We carry out projects from conception to actualization, with excellent client services, the best place to invest your finances. Aveela Groups Inc. was conceived in 2021 as a private Company and commenced business that same year. The Company started as a small but disciplined entity working mainly as a web design service,engineer services and HSE Consultants; then as a small contracts handler and is gradually growing until today. From the beginning, Aveela Groups started out as a civil engineering/HSE company; today, it is still growing to become a major player in the building profession, and to be responsible for some unmistakable landmarks.
